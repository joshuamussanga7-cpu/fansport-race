RULES OF THE RACE

  • 1. Promotion Period: February 1st - February 28th, 2025.
  • 2. Open to all registered FanSport affiliates in Tanzania.
  • 3. Minimum qualification: 50 New First Time Depositors (FTDs) within the month.
  • 4. Each FTD must make a minimum deposit of $2 to be counted towards the race.
  • 5. Affiliates are ranked based on the Total Deposit Volume generated by their qualifying FTDs.
  • 6. Total Prize Fund: $5,000 to be shared among the top 10 winners.
  • 7. Prize Breakdown: 1st ($2,000), 2nd ($1,200), 3rd ($800), 4th-10th ($1,000 total).
  • 8. Any form of incentive traffic or multi-accounting will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • 9. Rewards will be paid directly into the affiliate's account by March 10th, 2025.
  • 10. FanSport management reserves the right to change or terminate the race at any time.
